Core concept

Make it feel like:

“A sketchbook of our story, brought to life.”

Dark-themed, colourful, hand-drawn/cartoon vibe, with gentle animations, little surprises, and a romantic but tasteful mood.
Less text, more moments, illustrations, motion, and interaction.

Website structure

Keep it to 5 sections, each like a comic scene.

1. Hero section — “Her special entrance”

First screen should feel emotional immediately.

What shows

“Happy Birthday Mariam” hand-drawn title

subtitle like:
“A little illustrated world made just for you.”

cartoon version of her or both of you

floating stars / hearts / sparkles

a button: “Open the story”

Animation

slow fade-in

sketch lines drawing themselves

stars twinkling

button pulses softly

1. “How it started”

A comic-strip timeline of your story.

Panels

2019: when you first started

long distance love

only meeting once back then

2022: separation because you were not ready

last year: she still checked up on you

now: you found your way back

Keep text very short
Example:

2019 — The beginning

Distance never erased what was real

2022 — Wrong time, true feelings

2024/2025 — She still cared

Now — A better heart, a clearer love

Animation

scroll-triggered comic panels

little arrows and doodles animate in

images slide in like sketchbook pages

1. Interactive memory section

This is where it becomes unforgettable.

Ideas:

Polaroid gallery of your photos

click each image to reveal a tiny note

“open envelope” interaction with a special message

Examples of hidden note titles

“What I never forgot”

“What I admire about you”

“Why this time is different”

1. Final section — “Letter + wish”

End with a handwritten-note style panel.

Very short, heartfelt.

Example heading:
“For Mariam, on your day”

Then 4–6 lines only:

You have always held a special place in my heart.
Time changed many things, but it never erased your truth.
Thank you for being kind, sincere, and unforgettable.
I’m grateful for you, and I pray this new year brings you joy, peace, and love.
Happy Birthday, Mariam. 🤍

Then add:

music toggle

“Replay story” button

“One last surprise” button opening a final animated scene
